About this site

This website was created as part of my ENGL 102 multimodal research project at the University of South Carolina. 

Goals

This site aims to make known to students the plentiful digital textbook options that are available to them. Digital textbooks are only growing and students should be ready to take advantage of their benefits.

The site contains information on textbook pricing as well as fast facts on why prices are the way that they are.
